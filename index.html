About This Calculator

This calculator is designed according to Singapore's water supply standards:

  • PUB (Public Utilities Board) Guidelines
  • SS 636:2018 - Code of Practice for Water Services
  • Building and Construction Authority (BCA) Requirements

Key Features:

  • Accurate consumption calculations based on building type
  • Peak hour demand factor consideration
  • Fire fighting reserve calculations (SCDF requirements)
  • Additional water requirements for special facilities
  • 3D visualization and detailed reporting

Version: 2.0.1

Last Updated: June 2025

Peak Hour Factors

Storage Requirements

  • Minimum: 12 hours of average daily demand
